About Me

In my artistic journey, I delve into the depths of my thoughts to channel personal emotions into creations that captivate and transform. Whether through pen and ink, watercolor, or acrylic, I meticulously explore the imperfections and unique details of objects, aiming to capture their profound charm. Inspired by the complexities of humanity and the beauty of nature, I reimagine ordinary subjects metaphorically, infusing them with my vision.

Embracing the playful and experimental nature of art, I've discovered the beauty in mistakes, leading to new styles and techniques. I've developed a fondness for adding long and extraneous lines to create a "bigger picture," where multiple themes emerge collectively. Additionally, I explore fashion design, analyzing trends to recreate runway pieces.

Presently, I am pursuing a degree in Visual Studio Arts, an endeavor supported by the esteemed Chancellor's Scholarship, a title I proudly hold among a select group of forty incoming students. This scholarship has afforded me the tools to expand my artistic knowledge, preparing me for future creative aspirations. My artistic journey is dedicated to illuminating the ordinary aspects of life that often go unnoticed, celebrating the shared beauty inherent in all of us.

Experienced in Microsoft Office applications, including Word, Excel, and PowerPoint, as well as proficiency in Adobe software such as Acrobat, InDesign, and Illustrator.

Recipient of UC San Diego's selective Chancellor's scholarship. Received $20,000 award for academic excellence.
